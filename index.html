Who We Are

About Team Ovrheat

Team Ovrheat Racing is a student-led STEM engineering team from The Ardee School, New Delhi, competing in STEM Racing India 2026. We are a unit built on precision, innovation, and the relentless pursuit of victory — here to prove what Indian STEM racing teams are truly capable of on the global stage.

Our Story

The name "Ovrheat" means exactly what it sounds like — we push past the point where others stop. We are thinkers, designers, and engineers who refuse to settle for average. We embrace being underestimated, using discipline, creativity, and teamwork to turn doubt into performance and ambition into results.

We exist to challenge misconceptions — to show that Indian STEM racing teams are capable of competing, and winning, on the global stage. We push past limits, rise under pressure, and intend to set a new standard for what teams like us can achieve.

We are the next generation of engineers and innovators from India. Every decision we make is driven by one goal: to prove that the institutions we represent can and will compete — and win — at the highest level.

What is STEM Racing?

STEM Racing is a global competition where student teams design, manufacture, test, and race miniature CO2-powered Formula 1 cars. Beyond engineering, teams develop real enterprise skills — from sponsorship and marketing to project management. Engineering excellence meets real-world business.

Engineering Excellence

The Car

Our car is the result of months of CAD modelling, CFD simulation, and iterative refinement — every design decision optimised for one purpose: competitive performance.

Design Philosophy

Our design process balances aerodynamic efficiency with structural integrity. CAD modelling and CFD analysis guide every iteration — reducing drag, maximising straight-line speed, and preserving stability through cornering.

Responsible Innovation

Sustainability

Sustainability is not a box-tick for Team Ovrheat — it is embedded in every decision we make. Our three-pillar framework ensures we compete responsibly and leave a meaningful legacy.

🌍

Environmental

Reducing our environmental footprint across design, manufacturing, and competition operations.

  • CNC waste target ≤15% through optimized design
  • Some recycled materials incorporated into pit display
  • Digital-first operations (zero internal print)
  • Lightweight engineering reduces material use
🎓

Social

Inspiring the next generation of engineers through education and community engagement.

  • Raising the profile of STEM and engineering at The Ardee School through our public competition journey
  • Diversity & inclusion across team recruitment
  • Sharing our progress openly with our school community
💰

Economic

Disciplined financial management that protects the programme and maximises value from every rupee.

  • Weekly budget review and variance tracking
  • In-kind sponsorship to reduce cash spend
  • CNC optimization minimizes re-cuts
  • Surplus funds ringfenced for nationals
  • ₹300/gram charity commitment integrated
