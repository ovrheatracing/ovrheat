Who We Are

About Team Ovrheat

Team Ovrheat Racing is a student-led engineering team from The Ardee School, New Delhi, competing in the F1 in Schools STEM Racing Competition. We combine performance engineering with enterprise innovation and professional project management.

Our Story

The name "Ovrheat" reflects our philosophy of "overheating effort" — committing relentless energy, creativity, and precision into every aspect of our work. From aerodynamic optimization and manufacturing to branding and sponsorship outreach, we strive to exceed expectations through constant iteration and improvement.

We embrace the identity of the "dark horses." Rather than relying on reputation alone, our focus is on disciplined preparation, innovative thinking, and strategic execution to break through expectations and compete at the highest level.

As a team, we represent the next generation of engineers and innovators: individuals who combine technical excellence, creativity, and professional collaboration to transform ambitious ideas into real performance.

What is F1 in Schools?

F1 in Schools is a global STEM racing competition where student teams design, manufacture, test, and race miniature Formula 1 cars powered by CO2 cartridges. Beyond engineering, teams develop enterprise skills including sponsorship acquisition, marketing, and professional project management. It's the ultimate combination of performance engineering and real-world business excellence.

Engineering Excellence

The Car

Every curve, every surface, every design decision is optimized for one thing: speed. Our car represents countless hours of CAD modeling, CFD simulation, and iterative refinement.

Design Philosophy

Our design balances aerodynamic efficiency with structural integrity. Using advanced CAD software and computational fluid dynamics (CFD), we've optimized every millimeter to reduce drag and maximize straight-line speed while maintaining stability through turns.

Responsible Innovation

Sustainability

Sustainability is not a box-tick for Team Ovrheat — it is embedded in every decision we make. Our three-pillar framework ensures we compete responsibly and leave a meaningful legacy.

🌍

Environmental

Minimizing our footprint at every stage of design, manufacturing, and competition.

  • CNC waste target ≤15% through optimized design
  • 100% recycled pit display materials
  • Digital-first operations (zero internal print)
  • Local sourcing within Delhi NCR
  • Lightweight engineering reduces material use
🎓

Social

Inspiring the next generation of engineers through education and community engagement.

  • Full-school keynote to 600+ students
  • Curriculum integration (Physics, Math, DT)
  • Peer mentorship for younger students
  • Diversity & inclusion across recruitment
  • Open community demonstration days
💰

Economic

Financial discipline that protects our mission and ensures long-term sustainability.

  • Weekly budget review and variance tracking
  • In-kind sponsorship to reduce cash spend
  • CNC optimization minimizes re-cuts
  • Surplus funds ringfenced for nationals
  • ₹300/gram charity commitment integrated
